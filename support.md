# Tempo Support

Tempo is built by an independent developer in Australia. The fastest way to get help is email.

**Contact:** [tempo.studios@proton.me](mailto:tempo.studios@proton.me)

You can usually expect a reply within 1–2 business days.

## Common questions

### How do I cancel my Tempo+ subscription?

iOS handles subscriptions, not the app itself. Open the Settings app → tap your name at the top → **Subscriptions** → **Tempo+** → **Cancel Subscription**. You can also reach this from inside Tempo: **Settings → Tempo+ → Manage Subscription**.

Cancelling lets your subscription run to the end of the period you've already paid for, then stops. Your calendar, tasks, and everything you've created remain intact — Tempo+ features just turn off.

### How do I restore a purchase on a new device?

Make sure you're signed in to the same Apple ID you bought Tempo with, then in Tempo open **Settings → Tempo+ → Restore Purchases**.

### The app crashed / something looks broken

Email me at [tempo.studios@proton.me](mailto:tempo.studios@proton.me) with:

- Your iPhone model and iOS version (Settings → General → About)
- Tempo's version (Settings → bottom of the list)
- What you were doing when it happened
- A screenshot if you can grab one

### A request, an idea, something you wish Tempo did

I read everything. [tempo.studios@proton.me](mailto:tempo.studios@proton.me).
